Paste your save into this box to get recommendations for heirloom upgrades.

Currently the only mods this will recommend upgrading are Trimp Attack, Crit Chance, Crit Damage, Plaguebringer, Void Map Drop Chance, Fluffy Exp, and Miner Efficiency. If you have other useful mods on your heirlooms (such as Explorer Efficiency or Fragment Drop Rate), you'll have to upgrade them based on feel, as they're very difficult to weigh.

Additionally, this will not recommend replacing mods with stronger ones, nor adding mods in empty slots.

VM weight and XP weight are multipliers to the value of the Void Map Drop Chance and Fluffy Exp mods respectively. So if your next VMDC upgrade were to increase your expected helium by 0.5%, a weight of 12 would multiply it by 12, making it calculated as if it were to increase your value by 6%.

Weapon levels is the number of weapon levels you have by the end of your run.

Portal zone is the zone you're going to portal at, and Void zone is the zone you're going to run your void maps at.

If any of the bottom 3 inputs are blank, your recommended purchase will likely be incorrect, so you should fill them out.

A toggle for legacy mode can be found in the bottom right corner. This resorts to the old VMDC/Fluffy exp calculations directly from nSheetz's spreadsheet, which can be found by clicking here, and does not use the Portal zone/Void zone inputs. Legacy mode is not recommended, but if you're using it, VM/XP weights of 12/11.25 respectively are recommended.
